Our Story

Born from the frustration of forgotten assignments and missed deadlines.

How MyHomework Began

MyHomework was created by a group of high school students who were tired of losing track of assignments across five different classes. Every week felt like a guessing game — what's due tomorrow? Did I turn that in? What's my grade right now?

We built the tool we wished we had: a simple, clean platform that puts all your academic responsibilities in one easy-to-read dashboard.

Our Mission

To give every student the clarity, confidence, and organization they need to succeed academically — no matter their school, grade level, or learning style.
